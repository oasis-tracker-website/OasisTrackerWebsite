About Oasis

Oasis is a NetherGames Guild that specializes in the gamemode, "The Bridge."
It is notorious for having most of the bridge leaderboards members in it's guild of bridge,
as well as an active player base of bridge players.
Oasis was created on October 26th, 2020, around 2 months after the release of Bridge."
